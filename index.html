About Photo Recover

Photo Recover is a user-friendly application designed to help you retrieve accidentally deleted photos, videos, and other files from your device.

Key Features

  • 📷 Photo Recovery – Scan and attempt to recover deleted images from your device's storage.
  • 🎥 Video Recovery – Supports recovery of video files in various formats.
  • 📄 File Recovery – Restore documents and other file types (availability depends on device storage conditions).
  • 🔍 View Recovered Files – Browse and manage successfully recovered files within the app.
